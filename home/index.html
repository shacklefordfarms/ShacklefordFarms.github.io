About Us

Shackleford Farms is a company based in traditional values. We are a family company. Threfore, above all, family is the most important thing for us. As a result, we take care of our production really carefully; since this food will feed other families! We love what we do and we have being farming for over the past 150 years in the same land. Lately, we have being expading our business and buying other farms around our land. Nowadays we farm a total of 2087 acres; always aiming to produce a better quality product!
